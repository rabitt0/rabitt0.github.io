- Its been a really long time since I watched a happy ending, I couldn't even
remember the last time I saw one

textbookz, I'm not alleric to colorz I just forgot 'em, these dense worlds of
asano made me forget about how colorful, vibrant and happy media I used to
consume.
- I'm not criticizing asano, he does what he excels at, I just did it too much
and pretty extreme
- From the very beginning my eyez were mesmerized with this new and beautiful
world.
- Suzume's journey also felt very new as I was used to internal monologues,
the introspective ones, I wasn't able to think of external superficial journeys

suffocation but the freedom looks way too scarier and makes me feel more
excited of my own life when I'll become an adult n leave this cave.
